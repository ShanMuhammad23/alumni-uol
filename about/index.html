About Us

About Us

Welcome to University of Lahore (UOL) Alumni Portal- your central hub for all activities related to the UOL alumni community. Whether you are reconnecting with classmates, participating in networking events, staying updated with campus news or exploring exclusive alumni services, this portal is designed to keep you engaged and informed.

Message from Chairman

Awais Raoof

Dear Alumni,
Welcome to the UOL Alumni Portal. We are proud to have been part of your educational journey and value the bond that continues beyond graduation. Our alumni community, spread across the globe, is a source of pride, contributing to society, advancing professions, and carrying forward the legacy of UOL. This platform is designed to help you reconnect with peers, grow your professional network, and remain engaged with your alma mater. I encourage you to stay connected, participate in events, mentor students, and share your success stories. Your involvement strengthens both our community and the future of UOL. This is your second home. Welcome back!

Message from Rector

Prof. Dr. Muhammad Ashraf

It’s a pleasure to reach out to you through our alumni website. Every one of you has played a part in shaping the University of Lahore’s story, and your success continues to make us proud. This platform is here to help you stay connected — to share updates, join events, mentor students, or simply reconnect with old friends and the University community. We’d love to hear your stories and ideas on how we can grow together. Thank you for staying part of the UOL family.
