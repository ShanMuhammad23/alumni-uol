Alumni Association

The Alumni Association is at the heart of our alumni network. It serves as a platform for maintaining strong connections with your alma mater and fellow graduates. As a member, you will be part of a dynamic group that actively contributes to the growth and development of the university, organizes events and creates opportunities for alumni to engage with each other. Be part of something bigger by joining the UOL Alumni Association and make your voice heard in shaping our community.

Alumni Chapters

Our alumni chapters around the world create local networks that help keep the UOL spirit alive, no matter where our graduates are. These chapters organize regional events, foster professional and social connections and provide opportunities for alumni to engage with their peers and support university initiatives. They serve as vibrant communities that strengthen the global UOL alumni network.

Alumni Mentorship Program

The Alumni Mentorship Program bridges the gap between experienced professionals and fresh graduates or current students. Whether you want to share your career insights, offer guidance on academic pursuits or provide support for personal growth, becoming a mentor is a fulfilling way to give back. Your expertise can shape the future of our students and fellow alumni. Join us today and help guide the next generation of University of Lahore graduates toward success.

Distinguished Alumni

Our alumni community is filled with remarkable individuals making an impact across various industries. Whether you are excelling in your career, pursuing cutting-edge research or making strides in entrepreneurship, we want to celebrate your success! Share your achievements with us and inspire current students and fellow graduates. Join our Alumni Achievers network to gain recognition, share your story and connect with others making a difference. Let’s showcase your success as a source of pride for The University of Lahore.

Alumni In Sports

If you have a passion for sports and want to stay active while connecting with fellow alumni, Alumni in Sports is the place for you! Whether you played sports during your time at the university or want to get involved now, our program offers opportunities to participate in alumni sports events and fitness activities. Join us to continue your athletic journey, foster teamwork and build lasting friendships through sportsmanship. Let’s keep the spirit of UOL alive through active and fun participation!
