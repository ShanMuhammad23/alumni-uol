Local Chapters

UOL Alumni Local Chapters

Our local chapters bring together UOL alumni from regions across Pakistan, creating vibrant communities that foster learning, collaboration, and professional growth. Each chapter serves as a platform for networking, mentorship, and continuous development while strengthening the bonds between alumni and their alma mater.

1

Lahore & Surrounding Chapter

This chapter includes alumni from Lahore, Kasur, Kot Radha Kishen, Sharaqpur Sharif, Muridke, Narang Mandi, Sheikhupura, Chunian, Pattoki, Renala Khurd, Okara, Depalpur, Haveli Lakha, Hujra Shah Muqeem, and Manwala. Together, this diverse and vibrant region forms a strong alumni network that fosters learning, collaboration, and professional growth through shared experiences and opportunities.

Lahore Kasur Sheikhupura Okara

Gujranwala–Gujrat–Sialkot Chapter

This chapter brings together alumni from Gujranwala, Gujrat, Sialkot, Daska, Wazirabad, Kamoke, Hafizabad, Alipur Chattha, Lala Musa, and Kharian. Representing an important industrial and commercial belt, this chapter provides valuable networking, mentorship, and development opportunities for alumni.

Gujranwala Gujrat Sialkot Wazirabad
3

Faisalabad Chapter

The Faisalabad chapter includes alumni from Faisalabad, Jaranwala, Samundri, Gojra, Toba Tek Singh, Chiniot, and Jhang. With representation from one of Pakistan's major economic hubs, this chapter facilitates rich learning experiences, professional collaboration, and continuous growth for its members.

Faisalabad Jhang Toba Tek Singh Chiniot

Sargodha–Khushab Chapter

This chapter consists of alumni from Sargodha, Khushab, Jauharabad, Bhalwal, and Mianwali. These interconnected cities form a cohesive alumni community that supports peer learning, career development, and collective progress through regular engagement and knowledge-sharing.

Sargodha Khushab Mianwali Bhalwal

Multan Chapter

The Multan chapter covers alumni living in Multan, Khanewal, Lodhran, Vehari, and nearby areas such as Jahanian. This chapter represents a dynamic blend of professional fields and offers alumni meaningful opportunities for growth, leadership building, and networking.

Multan Khanewal Lodhran Vehari
6

Bahawalpur–Bahawalnagar Chapter

This chapter includes alumni from Bahawalpur, Bahawalnagar, Chishtian, Haroonabad, Hasilpur, and surrounding towns such as Fatehpur. It serves as a strong platform for alumni to connect, learn, and advance professionally while contributing to community development in the region.

Bahawalpur Bahawalnagar Chishtian Hasilpur
7

Sahiwal–Pakpattan Chapter

The Sahiwal chapter brings together alumni from Sahiwal, Pakpattan, Arifwala, Chichawatni, and Shorkot. This chapter fosters collaboration across education, business, and social sectors while promoting continuous learning and mutual growth among its members.

Sahiwal Pakpattan Arifwala Chichawatni

Southern Punjab Chapter

This chapter comprises alumni from Dera Ghazi Khan, Jampur, Rajanpur, Kot Addu, Layyah, Muzaffargarh, Jatoi, Liaqatpur, Rahim Yar Khan, and Sadiqabad. Spanning a broad yet connected region, it empowers alumni by building networks that encourage personal development, knowledge exchange, and career enhancement.

Dera Ghazi Khan Rahim Yar Khan Muzaffargarh Layyah

Islamabad–Rawalpindi Chapter

The Islamabad–Rawalpindi chapter consists of alumni from Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Wah Cantt, Tarbela Ghazi, Attock, Chakwal, Dina, and Jhelum. Located at the center of national activity, this chapter provides strong opportunities for professional advancement through interaction, mentorship, and innovative learning.

Peshawar & Northern KP Chapter

This chapter includes alumni from Peshawar, Kohat, Bannu, Batkhela, Buner, Parachinar, Abbottabad, and Mansehra. Bringing together graduates across northern Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, this chapter promotes academic enrichment, leadership growth, and meaningful professional connections.

Kashmir Chapter

Sindh Chapter

This chapter comprises alumni from Karachi and Ghotki, representing both the metropolitan center and upper Sindh. It serves as a bridge for alumni across diverse industries, offering opportunities for career growth, networking, and continuous professional development.
